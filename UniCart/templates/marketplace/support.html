Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing?

To create a listing, you need to be registered as a seller. Go to your profile, enable seller mode, then click "Create New Listing" on the listings page.

How do I contact a seller?

When viewing a listing, you can see the seller's university and username. Contact them through your university email.

Can I edit my listing?

Yes, you can edit or delete your listings at any time from your profile page or the listing detail page.
